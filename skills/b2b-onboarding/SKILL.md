## Quality Standards

1. **No placeholders in final output**: All content must be complete and ready to use
2. **Language consistency**: One document, one language (all-English or all-Chinese)
3. **Industry-specific**: All content adapted to user's actual products and industry
4. **Visual-ready**: Content designed to work with the images/materials user has
5. **Actionable**: Every document should have clear next steps
6. **Professional quality**: Business-appropriate tone and formatting

## Common Pitfalls

1. **Generic content**: Without specific product info, generate industry-appropriate templates but flag what needs customization
2. **Missing visuals**: Generated text must be paired with user's actual photos/videos
3. **Overwhelming output**: Present deliverables in logical order, not all at once
4. **Unrealistic timelines**: Onboarding is a starting point, not instant results
5. **Missing follow-up**: The templates are tools — success depends on consistent execution
